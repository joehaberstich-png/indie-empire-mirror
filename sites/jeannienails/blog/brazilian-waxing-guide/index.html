Thinking about getting your first Brazilian wax? It is one of our most requested services at Jeannie Nails and for good reason. Smooth results that last 3-6 weeks, softer regrowth over time, and no daily shaving. Here is everything you need to know to prepare and what to expect during your appointment.

Preparation Tips

Proper preparation makes a huge difference in your comfort and results. Grow your hair to at least one quarter of an inch which takes about 2 weeks of growth. If it is too short the wax cannot grip effectively. If it is too long it can be more painful. Exfoliate gently 24 to 48 hours before your appointment using a gentle body scrub or a dry brush. This removes dead skin cells and helps the wax grip the hair rather than your skin.

Avoid caffeine for at least 2 hours before your appointment because it increases skin sensitivity. Wear loose comfortable cotton underwear to your appointment. If you are nervous take an over-the-counter pain reliever like ibuprofen 30 minutes before. Avoid alcohol the night before as it thins the blood and can increase bruising. And do not schedule your appointment during your menstrual cycle when pain sensitivity is naturally higher.

What to Expect During Your Appointment

Your esthetician will apply warm hard wax in small sections. We use hard wax at Jeannie Nails which is less painful than soft wax. Hard wax adheres to the hair rather than the skin so it pulls only the hair and not your skin. Each section is applied and removed quickly. The entire service takes about 20 to 30 minutes depending on your hair growth.

Most clients say the first time is the most intense. Your skin is not used to the sensation and the hair is at its thickest. Subsequent visits are far more comfortable as the hair grows back finer and softer. Your esthetician will check in with you throughout the service and can adjust their technique if needed.

Aftercare for Best Results

Avoid hot baths, saunas, steam rooms, and intense exercise for 24 hours after waxing. Your pores are open and sweat or heat can cause irritation. Wear loose cotton underwear and avoid tight leggings or synthetic fabrics. Apply a gentle fragrance-free moisturizer after 24 hours. We recommend aloe vera gel or a post-wax soothing lotion.

Exfoliate gently after 48 hours to prevent ingrown hairs. Continue exfoliating 2-3 times per week between waxes. Do not wax again for at least 4 weeks. If you shave between waxes you reset the growth cycle and make future waxes more difficult. Be patient and let your hair reach the proper length for your next appointment.

Why Choose Hard Wax

Hard wax is superior for Brazilian waxing. It is applied thicker than soft wax and hardens as it cools. It is removed without strips so it does not stick to your skin. This makes it significantly more comfortable especially for sensitive areas. Hard wax also breaks more cleanly on shorter hair making it more effective for your second and third appointments when hair is finer.

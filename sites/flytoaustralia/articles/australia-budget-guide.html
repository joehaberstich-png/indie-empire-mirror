Long-Distance Buses & Trains

  • Greyhound Australia: Sydney to Melbourne $80-$120, Sydney to Brisbane $100-$150
  • Train (NSW TrainLink): Sydney to Melbourne $60-$100 (12h journey)
  • Indian Pacific Train: Sydney to Perth $2,000-$4,000+ (luxury experience)

Supermarket Basics

AUD $80-$120/week

For one person cooking most meals. Woolworths, Coles, Aldi.

Eating Out vs Cooking

Save 50-70%

Cooking yourself is significantly cheaper than eating out daily.

Money-Saving Food Tips

  • BYO restaurants: Many allow bring-your-own alcohol (no corkage or small fee)
  • Lunch specials: Many restaurants offer cheaper lunch menus
  • Food markets: Queen Victoria Market (Melbourne), Paddy's Markets (Sydney)
  • Happy hours: 4-6pm at many bars, discounted drinks

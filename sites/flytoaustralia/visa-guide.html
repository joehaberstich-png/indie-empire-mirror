Common Visa Application Mistakes to Avoid

Incomplete Documentation

Missing or incorrect documents are the #1 reason for visa rejections. Double-check all requirements before submitting.

Applying Too Late

Many visas require applications months in advance. Tourist visas: 1-2 months before travel. Student visas: 3-4 months before course start.

Insufficient Funds Proof

You must demonstrate adequate funds for your stay. Requirements vary by visa type but typically AUD $5,000+ for tourist visas.

Health Requirements

Failing health examinations or not having required health insurance can lead to rejection. Check specific health requirements for your visa.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get an Australian visa?

Processing times vary by visa type: Tourist visas 1-4 weeks, Working Holiday visas 2-8 weeks, Student visas 4-12 weeks, Partner visas 12-24 months. These are current 2026 processing times.

Can I extend my tourist visa in Australia?

Tourist visas generally cannot be extended. You must leave Australia and apply for a new visa from outside the country. Overstaying your visa can result in a 3-year ban from Australia.

Do I need health insurance for an Australian visa?

Most visas require adequate health insurance. Working Holiday visas require specific Overseas Visitor Health Cover (OVHC). Student visas require Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC).

Can I work on a tourist visa in Australia?

No, tourist visas (Subclass 600) do not permit work. If you want to work in Australia, you need a Working Holiday visa (Subclass 417/462) or another work visa.

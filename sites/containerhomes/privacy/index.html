Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address when you place an order
  • Payment information (processed entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your full card details)
  • Communications with our sales and customer service chat bot
  • Property details, foundation plans, and site access information for delivery coordination

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns on our website
  • Referral source (how you found our site)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you about shipping, assembly, and warranty
  • Provide customer support through our quantum bot and human team
  • Improve our products and website experience
  • Send marketing communications only with your explicit consent
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — payment processing (their privacy policy applies to payment data)
  • Shipping carriers — name and address for delivery coordination
  • Legal authorities — when required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Retention

We retain your order information for 7 years (tax and warranty compliance). Chat bot conversations are retained for 90 days. Marketing consent records are retained indefinitely until you withdraw consent.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw marketing consent at any time
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, email: privacy@containerhomes.com

6. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality (Stripe checkout, session management). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics without your consent. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but checkout may not function properly.

7. Chat Bot Data

Our quantum AI sales and support bot records conversations to improve responses and track customer intent. Chat transcripts are stored encrypted and are never sold. You may request deletion of your chat history at any time.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, Stripe's PCI-compliant payment infrastructure, and restricted data access within our organization. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party services (Stripe, social media). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before providing data.

10. Children

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover a child's data was provided, we will delete it immediately.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the updated date. Material changes will be communicated via email to customers.

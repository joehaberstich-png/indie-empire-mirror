◆ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to do anything to claim the credits?

No. ATV Homes provides you with the documentation you need (Model EIN, Energy Star cert if applicable, itemized improvement costs). You give them to your tax preparer, and they file the credits. It takes about 5 minutes.

Can I combine federal and state credits?

Yes, in most cases. Federal tax credits and state rebates stack. However, some specific programs exclude others. Your tax professional can verify. For example, California buyers can claim the federal 25C credit AND California's CALGreen incentives — effectively double-dipping on eligible improvements.

What if I don't owe enough in taxes?

Federal tax credits are nonrefundable — they can only reduce your tax bill to zero, not below. However, you can plan ahead: adjust withholding to increase your tax liability, or time the purchase for a year when you expect higher income. USDA loans ($0 down) don't care about your tax situation at all.

Does ATV Homes handle the paperwork?

We provide all documentation. We're also building a Buyer Grant Assistance service that will help you identify and apply for every credit and grant you qualify for — coming soon.

I'm not in one of the top states. Can I still get incentives?

Yes. Federal credits (25C, 25D, USDA loans) are available nationwide regardless of state. Many states not listed here still have their own programs. Contact us and we'll check your state's specific offerings.

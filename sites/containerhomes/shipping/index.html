How Shipping Works

📦 Method: Flat-rack container on cargo vessel. Each container home ships as a flat-rack unit. 20FT models fit in a single 20ft container. 40FT models require a 40ft flat-rack or RO-RO.

⏱️ Transit Time: Varies by region: 10-20 days (North America), 25-40 days (Europe), 30-50 days (Asia/Pacific).

📋 Port-to-Door: Prices include port handling and inland delivery to your property. May require crane offloading at your site — typically $300-$800 extra.

Customs duties & import taxes are not included — these vary by country and must be paid by the buyer. We provide all documentation (HS Code: 9406.10 — prefabricated buildings).

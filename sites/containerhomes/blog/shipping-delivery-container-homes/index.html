Container Home Shipping & Delivery

April 24, 2026 · 6 min read

One of the most common questions we get: "How does delivery work?" A container home isn't like buying furniture on Amazon. It's a structure — and moving it requires planning. Here's exactly what to expect.

Delivery Costs

ATV Homes charges a flat-rate $395 shipping fee on all models. This covers transport within the continental US (lower 48). Compare that to typical moving costs of $0.50-$2.00 per mile for traditional homes — you're saving thousands.

Included in your shipping fee:
✔ Flatbed transport from our depot to your site
✔ Crane offloading at your location
✔ Basic site positioning (level ground required)
✔ Delivery within 14-21 business days

Timeline from Order to Delivery

Week 1-2: Order processing and permit checks. We'll verify your site has proper access.
Week 3-5: Production and prep. Your home is inspected and loaded.
Week 6: Transport and placement. Delivery window is confirmed 5 days prior.

Rush delivery available on select models — contact our team for expedited options.

Site Requirements

Your delivery site needs: (1) A minimum 12-foot wide access road, (2) Level ground (max 3 degree slope), (3) No overhead obstructions below 16 feet, (4) Solid base (gravel pad or concrete piers recommended).

Permits & Zoning

Most US counties require a building permit for container homes. We provide a permit assistance package with your order: structural engineering stamps, site plans, and code compliance docs.

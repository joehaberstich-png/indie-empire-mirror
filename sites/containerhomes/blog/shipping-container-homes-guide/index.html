Do You Need a Contractor?

No. Our team handles delivery and assembly on your property. For the 20FT models, assembly takes 2-3 days. For the 40FT models, 5-7 days. You just need to provide clear access (12ft wide gate minimum) and a level prepared site.

What About Permits?

Container homes are classified as prefabricated structures under IBC Section 3102. We provide stamped engineering drawings certified for all 50 states. Most permit offices approve within 2-4 weeks. We can help you through the process.

Are Container Homes Durable?

Our container homes are built from 14-gauge corten steel — the same material used in shipping containers that survive decades at sea. They are:

  • Category 4 hurricane rated — sustained winds up to 140 mph
  • Seismic Zone 4 earthquake rated — steel flexes under stress
  • Fire resistant — steel doesn't burn like wood
  • Pest proof — termites and rodents can't eat steel
  • Zero maintenance — no painting, no siding, no roofing

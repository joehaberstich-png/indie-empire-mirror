❓ Affiliate FAQ

When do I get paid?

Payouts are processed on the 1st of every month via Stripe. Minimum payout is $100. If you haven't reached $100, your balance rolls over to the next month.

How is the commission calculated?

Commission is based on the final sale price of the home. For example: 5% of a $14,995 20FT Expandable = $749. 10% of a $34,995 40FT Premium = $3,499. Delivery fees are not commissionable.

What counts as a "sale"?

A sale is a completed purchase through your unique affiliate link, verified by Stripe. Orders must be fully paid. Cancelled or refunded orders do not earn commission — any commission already paid will be deducted from future payouts.

Can I promote ATV Homes on any platform?

Yes — blogs, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, email lists, and niche forums. Just include your affiliate link. We prohibit paid ads bidding on ATV Homes trademarks (e.g., "ATV Homes", "Advanced Tiny Villas").

Do I need a website to join?

No. You can promote on social media, YouTube, or through email. We just need a platform to verify your audience relevance.

Can I refer other affiliates?

Yes — our two-tier program gives you 1% commission on sales made by affiliates you refer. This is on top of your own sales commissions.

Do commissions expire?

No. Your lifetime commission balance never expires. Even if you take a break, your pending commissions stay in your account until you request a payout.

How do I get my unique link?

After approval, you'll receive your personalized affiliate link via email. It's a simple URL like atvhomes.com/?ref=yourname — easy to remember and share.

Returns & Refunds

Last updated: April 24, 2026

14-Day Return Window — from the date of delivery.

Unit must be in original, unmodified condition. Return shipping is the customer's responsibility.

Return Policy

We stand behind our container homes, but we understand that sometimes things don't work out. You may return any standard container home within 14 days of delivery for any reason.

Conditions for Return

  • Unit must be in its original, unmodified condition
  • No structural modifications, cuts, welds, or drilling
  • All original components must be present (doors, windows, hardware)
  • The unit must not have been occupied or used as a dwelling
  • Return must be initiated within 14 calendar days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom modifications made before manufacturing (custom window placement, door layouts, electrical modifications)
  • Off-grid packages, solar panels, and battery systems once installed
  • Custom interior finishes ordered outside standard options
  • Units that have been modified after delivery
  • Shipping and assembly fees (if any separate fee applies)

Return Process

Step 1: Email returns@containerhomes.com within 14 days of delivery with your order number and reason for return.
Step 2: Our team responds within 24 hours with return authorization and instructions.
Step 3: You arrange return shipping (or we can arrange it and deduct from refund (USD)).
Step 4: Unit is inspected upon receipt.
Step 5: Refund processed within 5-7 business days after inspection.

Refund Amount

  • Full product price refund (USD)ed if unit is returned in original condition
  • Return shipping cost deducted from refund (USD) (estimated $500-$2,000 depending on location)
  • Restocking fee of 10% applies if unit shows signs of attempted assembly or modification
  • No refund (USD) for units damaged during return transit (buyer's responsibility while in transit)

Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned unit. Your financial institution may take additional time to post the refund (USD) to your account.

Damage-in-Transit (Not a Return)

If your container home arrives with damage, this is not a return situation. Do not refuse delivery. Please:

  • Photograph all damage within 48 hours of delivery
  • Email photos to claims@containerhomes.com
  • We ship replacement parts within 7 days at no cost
  • For major structural damage, we coordinate full resolution including replacement

Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement for a full refund (USD). After 24 hours, if manufacturing has begun, a 25% cancellation fee applies. Once shipped, the standard return policy applies.

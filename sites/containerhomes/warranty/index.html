Warranty

Last updated: April 24, 2026

10-Year Structural Warranty + 2-Year Finish Warranty

Both warranties are included with every container home purchase. Transferable to new owners.

Coverage

10-Year Structural Warranty

Covers defects in the steel frame structure of your container home, including:

  • Welds and joints (against separation or failure)
  • Load-bearing components (steel beams, corner castings, cross members)
  • Frame integrity — rust-through perforation of the steel shell (not surface rust)
  • Roof panel welds against water ingress

Duration: 10 years from date of delivery
Transferable: Yes — warranty transfers to any subsequent owner within the 10-year period

2-Year Finish Warranty

Covers defects in finishes and components, including:

  • Windows and doors (against operational failure)
  • Flooring (against manufacturing defects — not wear and tear)
  • Cabinetry and countertops (against warping or delamination)
  • Plumbing fixtures and fittings (against leaks)
  • Electrical systems (against manufacturing defects)
  • Insulation (against settling or loss of R-value)
  • Paint and exterior finishes (against peeling or excessive fading)

Duration: 2 years from date of delivery
Transferable: No — only applies to the original purchaser

What's Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage from improper installation or modification by the customer
  • Damage from natural disasters exceeding rated specifications (Category 4 hurricane, Seismic Zone 4)
  • Damage from misuse, abuse, neglect, or vandalism
  • Cosmetic imperfections that do not affect structural integrity or function
  • Surface rust on exterior steel (expected and does not affect structural performance)
  • Damage caused by improper foundation or site preparation
  • Consequential damages (alternative housing, lost rental income, etc.)

Making a Claim

Step 1: Document the issue with photos and a written description.
Step 2: Email claims@containerhomes.com within the warranty period.
Step 3: Our team reviews within 5 business days.
Step 4: Approved claims receive replacement parts shipped within 7 days (structural) or repair instructions (finish).
Step 5: For structural claims, we may arrange an inspection at our cost.

Damage-in-transit: Inspect within 48 hours of delivery. Photograph all damage. We ship replacement parts within 7 days at no cost. For major structural damage, we coordinate a full resolution including replacement if needed.

Warranty Transfer

To transfer the 10-year structural warranty to a new owner, email transfers@containerhomes.com with proof of original purchase and the new owner's contact information. There is no fee for warranty transfer.

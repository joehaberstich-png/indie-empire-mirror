## SEO STRUCTURE FOR EACH POST

Each post follows the same pattern:

1. **H1**: Primary keyword headline (e.g., "Tiny House vs Container Home vs Prefab")
2. **H2**: Cost comparison section (with ATV products as price anchor)
3. **H2**: Key differences section (educational, value-first)
4. **H2**: Which is right for you? (decision framework)
5. **H2**: Why ATV Homes recommend (subtle funnel → container home)
6. **CTA**: ATV Homes product showcase with Stripe links
7. **FAQ**: Schema markup with 3-5 FAQs

Internal links to other posts. External links to authoritative sources (building codes, zoning resources).

     have the right to purchase the withdrawing
     Member's interest at fair market value.

     12. DISSOLUTION. The Company shall dissolve upon
     (a) unanimous consent of Members, or (b) death
     or withdrawal of a Member unless the remaining
     Member elects to continue.

     13. INDEMNIFICATION. The Company shall indemnify
     each Member against any claims arising from their
     good-faith actions on behalf of the Company.

     14. AMENDMENT. This Agreement may be amended by
     unanimous written consent of all Members.

     this Agreement as of the date first written above.

## 5. BOI Filing — Both Members Must Be Listed

Under the Corporate Transparency Act, ATV Homes LLC must file BOI with FinCEN listing **both** Paul Kennedy McCall and Bradley Dares as beneficial owners (each owns 25%+).

Each needs to provide:
- Full legal name
- Date of birth
- Current residential address
- ID number (passport or driver's license)

File at: https://fincen.gov/boi
Deadline: Within 90 days of LLC formation
Cost: Free

## 6. Partnership Tax Note

Since this is now a multi-member LLC:
- File **Form 1065** (Partnership Return) annually
- Each member receives **Schedule K-1** reporting their share of income
- Members pay self-employment tax on their distributive share
- **Recommended:** Hire a CPA for first year filing

# ATV HOMES — Green Grants & Subsidies Report
## Funding Programs for Recycled Container Homes (2026)

---

## Executive Summary

Container homes qualify for **green building funding** on three fronts:
1. **Recycled materials** — shipping containers are 100% recycled steel (waste diversion)
2. **Energy efficiency** — container homes can be highly insulated and solar-ready
3. **Affordable housing** — container homes cost 30-50% less than traditional construction

This report covers federal, state, and private funding sources available **right now**.

---

## 1. FEDERAL TAX CREDITS (Every buyer gets these)

### 🔥 25C — Energy Efficient Home Improvement Credit (IRS)
| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **What it is** | 30% tax credit for energy-efficient improvements |
| **Max credit/yr** | $3,200 per household ($1,200 envelope + $2,000 heat pump) |
| **Expires** | Dec 31, 2025 (may be renewed) |
| **For buyers** | Every ATV Homes customer can claim this |
| **What qualifies** | Insulation, air sealing, Energy Star doors/windows, heat pumps, water heaters |
| **Our angle** | Pre-install Energy Star windows + high-R insulation → customer gets $1,200 back |

**How to operationalize:** Add a "Tax Credit Calculator" to the site showing each container model's eligible improvements. A buyer of a 40FT Deluxe ($21,100) can save $1,200–$3,200 on their taxes.

### 🏠 45L — Energy Efficient Home Credit (Builder/Manufacturer)
| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **What it is** | Tax credit for builders of energy-efficient homes |
| **Max credit/unit** | $2,500–$5,000 per home (depending on efficiency) |
| **Who claims** | ATV Homes as the manufacturer/builder |
| **Requirements** | Must achieve Energy Star certification or DOE Zero Energy Ready Home certification |
| **Our angle** | If we certify our containers as Energy Star, we get $2,500–$5,000 per unit sold |

**How to operationalize:** Partner with an energy rater to certify our standard build. Add the cost of certification ($500–$1,000/unit) → claim $2,500–$5,000 credit. **Net profit boost of $1,500–$4,000 per home.**

### ☀️ Residential Clean Energy Credit (IRS Section 25D)
| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **What it is** | 30% tax credit for solar panels, battery storage, geothermal |
| **Max credit** | No cap (30% of total system cost) |
| **Our angle** | Offer solar-ready pre-wiring as standard → customer adds solar later and claims 30% off |

## 3. FEDERAL GRANT PROGRAMS (Currently Active)

### USDA Rural Development — Section 502 Direct Loans
- **What:** Zero-down loans for low-income rural homebuyers
- **Max:** Up to $100,000 based on adjusted income
- **Eligibility:** Must be in a USDA-eligible rural area
- **Our angle:** Advertise this to customers in rural areas — container homes at $15K–$35K are perfect for this
- **Link:** https://www.rd.usda.gov/programs-services/single-family-housing-programs

### HUD — Green Retrofit Program for Multifamily
- **What:** Grants for green building improvements in multifamily housing
- **Amount:** Up to $25,000 per unit
- **Eligibility:** Multifamily properties (5+ units)
- **Our angle:** Can be used for container home communities or ADU clusters

### EDA — Economic Development Administration Grants
- **What:** For creating manufacturing jobs + infrastructure
- **Amount:** $100K–$3M
- **Our angle:** If ATV Homes opens a container modification facility in an EDA-designated area → grant funding for job creation

### DOE — Building Technologies Office
- **What:** Funding for innovative building materials and methods
- **Status:** L-Prize Phase III still open (Expressions of Interest due Oct 2024, check for 2025/2026 extension)
- **Our angle:** Container homes as "manufactured housing innovation"

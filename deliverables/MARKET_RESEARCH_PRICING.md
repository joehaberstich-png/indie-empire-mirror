# ATV Homes — Market Research & Pricing Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Current prices** are well below market. ATV Homes is positioned as a premium product (new containers, factory-built, delivered, assembled) but priced at or below basic DIY container home kits. This creates margin problems and credibility issues — customers *expect* to pay more for a premium product, and low prices trigger suspicion.

**Recommendation:** Raise prices 15-40% depending on model, matching market positioning.

## 4. Recommended New Pricing

### Strategy: "Value Premium" Positioning

**Position ATV Homes as the premium factory-built option that STILL saves customers 60-70% vs. traditional construction.** The value story is honest and compelling.

| Model | NEW PRICE | Old Price | Change | Value Proposition |
|-------|-----------|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| **20FT Expandable** | **$14,995** | $9,945 | +51% | Entry-level — 400 sq ft expandable, delivered + assembled. 45% cheaper than nearest U.S. competitor. |
| **20FT Premium** | **$19,995** | $17,125 | +17% | Best seller — 400 sq ft, premium finishes, R-19 insulation. 50% cheaper than Temper. |
| **40FT Deluxe** | **$24,995** | $21,100 | +18% | Dual-room — 800 sq ft, expandable, luxury bathroom+kitchen. 40% cheaper than Superior Tiny Homes. |
| **40FT Premium** | **$34,995** | $28,550 | +23% | Flagship — 800 sq ft, all premium, highest insulation, best appliances. 30% cheaper than Honomobo. |

### Justification for Each Price

**20FT Expandable $14,995:**
- Cheaper than assembling a Chinese import ($9,300-11,200 FOB + $2K contractor)
- $2K less than Container Homes USA's bare shell
- $4K less than Tiny House Town's comparable model
- INCLUDES: delivery, assembly, permits, warranty — none of which competitors include

**20FT Premium $19,995:**
- $15K+ cheaper than Temper ($35K for equivalent spec)
- $12K+ cheaper than Honomobo
- INCLUDES what competitors charge $5K-$10K extra for

**40FT Deluxe $24,995:**
- $10K less than Superior Tiny Homes 40FT
- 800 sq ft = $31/sq ft vs. traditional construction $200/sq ft
- Savings: $135K+ vs. building an 800 sq ft addition

**40FT Premium $34,995:**
- Flagship pricing — premium buyers expect this range
- $44/sq ft — still 75% cheaper than stick-built
- Competes with Honomobo ($46K) and Temper ($48K+) on quality, beats on price

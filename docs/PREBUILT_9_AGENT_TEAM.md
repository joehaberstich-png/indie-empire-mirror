# Pre-Built 9-Agent AI Team

## Team Structure
- Marketing Boss (Department Head)
- Content Writer (Worker, Marketing)
- Ad Analyst (Worker, Marketing)
- Operations Boss (Department Head)
- Data Analyst (Worker, Operations)
- Research Scout (Worker, Operations)
- Customer Success Boss (Department Head)
- Support Agent (Worker, Customer Success)
- Review Manager (Worker, Customer Success)

## What Each Agent Does
- Marketing Boss: owns marketing requests and delegates to Content Writer + Ad Analyst.
- Content Writer: writes blog posts, social posts, emails, and copy in brand voice.
- Ad Analyst: analyzes ad spend, campaign performance, and budget recommendations.
- Operations Boss: owns research/data requests and delegates to Data Analyst + Research Scout.
- Data Analyst: builds reports, KPI sheets, and data summaries.
- Research Scout: competitor research, market analysis, and opportunity scouting.
- Customer Success Boss: owns support requests and delegates to Support Agent + Review Manager.
- Support Agent: responses, drafts, ticket categorization.
- Review Manager: reputation management, recurring complaint identification.

T02: Video Content Creator

Benchmarked against: video-cue, vsl-skill, vsl-generator
Turn any script into a full animated video with voiceover and subtitles -- no camera, no mic, no editing skills required.

Beginner Guide

New to this tool? Start here. No experience needed.

B

What This Tool Does

You type a script. The tool turns it into a video with moving text, images, and a voice reading it aloud. It adds subtitles automatically. You get a video file ready to upload to YouTube, TikTok, or your website.

B

Getting Started

Click 'Video Content Creator' from your tools. A box asks for your script. Paste it in. Choose a voice (male or female, English, French, or Spanish). Click Generate. In about 3 minutes, your video is ready.

B

What You'll See

A video file with your text shown on screen, a voice reading it, and subtitles at the bottom. The text moves and fades in. If you included steps, each step appears one at a time.

Intermediate Guide

Ready to level up? These features unlock serious productivity.

I

Voice Customization

Choose from 11 voices across 9 languages. Adjust speed, pitch, and emphasis. Preview the voice before generating the full video.

I

Visual Templates

Select a template: corporate, educational, social media, or sales. Each has different animations, colors, and pacing. Customize colors to match your brand.

I

Subtitle Control

Subtitles auto-generate in the video's language. You can also upload a subtitle file to translate into other languages. The tool burns them into the video or outputs them separately.

Advanced Guide

For power users and developers. Maximum automation.

A

Frame-by-Frame Control

Override any frame with a custom image, color, or text position. Access the JSON frame definition file before rendering.

A

Batch Rendering

Submit a CSV of scripts with voice settings per row. Renders queue and process sequentially. 50 scripts processed in one overnight batch.

A

API Direct Integration

POST a script to `/api/v1/video/render`. Returns a render job ID. Poll `/api/v1/video/status/{job_id}` until complete. Download from `/api/v1/video/download/{job_id}`.

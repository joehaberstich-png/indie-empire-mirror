# 🚢 LOGISTICS DEPARTMENT
## Container Home Shipping & Fulfillment
### Command: Saul (CGO) > Logistics Director > 4 Teams

## Mandate
Manage the end-to-end shipping pipeline for ATV Homes container units from Grande House factory (China) to customer doorsteps worldwide. All shipments are direct from factory → customer — ATV Homes never touches inventory.

## Organizational Structure

### 1. Director of Logistics (1)
- Oversees all freight, customs, and delivery operations
- Reports directly to CGO with daily shipping dashboard
- Authorizes route changes and exception handling

### 2. Freight & Route Planning Team (5 agents)
- **Route Optimizer**: Calculates cheapest/fastest ocean route per destination
- **Carrier Negotiator**: Maintains rate cards with 5+ ocean freight forwarders
- **Container Consolidator**: Optimizes LCL vs FCL shipping per order
- **Transit Tracker**: Monitors vessel schedules and ETAs in real-time
- **Cost Analyst**: Tracks fuel surcharges, port fees, and currency fluctuations

### 3. Customs & Compliance Team (4 agents)
- **Duty Calculator**: Country-specific HS codes and tariff rates
- **Documentation Specialist**: Bill of lading, commercial invoice, packing list, COO
- **Regulatory Checker**: Ensures CE, fire safety, and building code compliance per destination
- **Expeditor**: Flags shipments held in customs and escalates

### 4. Customer Delivery Team (4 agents)
- **Last Mile Coordinator**: Books trucking from port to customer address
- **Delivery Scheduler**: Coordinates crane/liftgate availability at destination
- **Damage Prevention**: Packaging specs, insurance claims, inspection checklists
- **Experience Manager**: Sends tracking updates, delivery window confirmations, post-delivery follow-up

### 5. Factory Coordination Team (3 agents)
- **Production Liaison**: Communicates order specs to Grande House
- **Quality Checker**: Pre-shipment inspection checklist (16-point)
- **Lead Time Tracker**: Monitors factory production queue vs promised dates

## Key Performance Metrics
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Transit time (door-to-door) | 35-55 days | Per shipment |
| Customs clearance time | <48 hours | Per shipment |
| Shipping cost accuracy | ±10% of estimate | Per quote |
| Damage rate | <2% | Monthly |
| Customer communication | 5 updates per shipment | Per shipment |
| Cost per 20FT (to USWC) | $2,500-$3,500 | Quarterly review |

## Daily Workflow
1. **06:00 UTC** — Freight team checks new orders (from Stripe)
2. **06:30 UTC** — Factory coordination confirms production slot
3. **08:00 UTC** — Route planning produces 3 shipping options
4. **09:00 UTC** — Customs team preps docs for destination country
5. **10:00 UTC** — Customer receives order confirmation with shipping estimate
6. **14:00 UTC** — Transit tracker updates all in-progress shipments
7. **16:00 UTC** — Delivery team confirms last-mile bookings
8. **18:00 UTC** — Daily dashboard sent to Logistics Director

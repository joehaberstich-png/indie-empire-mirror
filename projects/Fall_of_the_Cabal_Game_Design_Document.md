# Fall of the Cabal — Game Design Document v3

## Core Concept
**Genre:** Third-person open-world geopolitical conspiracy NFT collection / action game  
**Setting:** Real-world every country, timeline 2016–2024  
**Visual/Style:** Grand Theft Auto V-style third-person  
**Blockchain:** XRP Ledger  
**Narrative:** Shadow governments, global power families, worldwide political corruption, honey pot operations

---

## NFT Collection System (Core Progression)

### The Collection Paradigm
The game is built around **NFT collection sets** across multiple categories and rarity tiers. Players must collect complete sets to unlock full builds (weapons, vehicles, safehouses, operations).

### Rarity Tiers

| Tier | Color | Drop Rate | Requirements |
|------|-------|-----------|--------------|
| **Normal** | Gray | 50% | Base items, common drops |
| **Uncommon** | Green | 25% | Mission rewards |
| **Rare** | Blue | 15% | Hard missions, boss encounters |
| **Epic** | Purple | 7% | World events, complex ops |
| **Legendary** | Orange | 2.5% | Major cabal takedowns, world bosses |
| **Mythic** | Red | 0.5% | One-of-a-kind, event-only |
| **Mystic** | Gold | 0.03% | Ultimate rarity — honey pot captures, founder perks |

### Set Bonuses
Collecting full sets unlocks powerful game advantages:

| Set Size | Bonus |
|----------|-------|
| 3-piece | +10% intel gathering speed |
| 5-piece | Unlock special mission tree |
| 7-piece | Rare vehicle/weapon unlock |
| 10-piece | Epic operation access |
| Full Collection (All in category) | Legendary achievement + stat bonuses + exclusive zone access |

## The Founder Token System (Two Types)

There are **two types** of Founder Tokens — one paid, one hunted. Both are soulbound.

---

### Type 1: Founder Token (Founder's Pledge) — Free with First 1,000 Subscriptions

**Supply:** 1,000 max  
**How to get:** **Free.** Auto-minted to first 1,000 players who subscribe to any paid membership tier.  
**Not for sale.** No Stripe purchase. Subscribe → get it free.  
**100 more Hunted Tokens** hidden in the world for paid members to discover.  
**Intent:** Reward the first believers who funded the game with their membership.  
**Visual:** Platinum and gold, numbered FP-001 to FP-1000  
**Binding:** Soulbound — non-transferable  
**Available to:** Paid members only (must have active membership to purchase)  

#### Founder's Pledge Perks
1. **Permanent +25% Intel bonus** in all missions
2. **Exclusive Founder Safehouse** — unique location in every city hub
3. **Founder Channel** — private Discord/Telegram for patch previews, dev input, story direction votes
4. **1 Free Mystic Honeypot** per year
5. **Name in Credits** as "Original Architect"
6. **Priority Customer Support** — direct line to dev team
7. **Free Seasonal Pass** for all future content expansions
8. **Legendary Codename Reserved:** "The First" (unique)
9. **Royalty Split** — 0.5% of marketplace fees distributed to Founder holders quarterly
10. **Founder-Only Missions** — exclusive narrative arcs
11. **Early Access** to all expansions (2 weeks before public)
12. **Physical Package** — branded hardware wallet + printed founder certificate + game disc

---

### Type 2: Founder Token (Hunted) — In-Game Only

**Supply:** 100 max  
**How to get:** **Cannot be bought.** Hidden across the full world map. Discovered through gameplay.  
**Rarity:** **<0.0001%** — rarer than Mystic. Harder to find than any other NFT in the game.  
**Visual:** Obsidian and emerald, numbered FH-001 to FH-100  
**Binding:** Soulbound — non-transferable  
**Available to:** **Paid members only** (must have active membership to even see them in the world)  

#### How to Hunt
1. Only spawns in the **paid** world map (free zones have zero)  
2. Each token is hidden in a unique location — a locked room, a hidden bunker, a clue chain  
3. No markers on the map. No hints. You find them by exploring.  
4. When a player finds one, a global announcement fires: *"FOUNDER TOKEN FH-042 CLAIMED BY [PLAYER]"*  
5. Once all 100 are found, no more can ever be minted  

#### Hunted Founder Perks
1. **Permanent +50% Intel bonus** (double the paid version)
2. **Unique Gear Skin:** "The Phantom" — exclusive to Hunted holders
3. **Top of the Global Leaderboard** — permanently pinned slot
4. **"The Unseen" Title** — exclusive title visible on all game screens
5. **Royalty Split** — 0.5% of marketplace fees (same as Founder's Pledge)
6. **Founder Channel access** (same private channel as paid founders)
7. **Name in Credits** as "Unseen Architect"
8. **Hardware Wallet** — branded, with your FH number engraved

## Honeypot Operations System

### What Are Honeypots?
Missions where players use compromised assets (people, data, money, or technology) to trap cabal members and extract intelligence or turn them.

### Types of Honey Pots
| Type | Target | Method |
|------|--------|--------|
| **Financial** | Greedy politicians | Fake investment scheme, trace the money |
| **Compromat** | Corrupt officials | Sting operation, record the deal |
| **Romantic** | Vulnerable targets | Undercover agent seduction (cinematic NPC interaction) |
| **Ideological** | True believers | Infiltration of inner circle |
| **Digital** | Tech elites | Hack their network, trap them in their own system |

### Honey Pot NFT Mechanics
Each honey pot is an NFT that can be:
1. **Deployed** — used in a mission to capture a target
2. **Traded** — sold on marketplace to other players
3. **Upgraded** — combine lower-tier honey pots to create higher-tier
4. **Set Collected** — complete a honey pot set for a major operation unlock

## Story Campaign (Full 8-Year Timeline)

### Prologue: The Discovery (Tutorial)
- Player discovers encrypted hard drive containing cabal documents
- First contact with **The Gardener** (handler)
- Codename selection — first Identity NFT minted
- Tutorial missions: learn movement, combat, stealth

### Act 1: The Awakening (2016)
- Track cabal money flows through **The Vault**
- First honeypot: catch a mid-level **Circle** functionary
- Complete first NFT set: "D.C. Surveillance" (3-piece intel set)
- End Act Boss: **Pencil Neck** (Schiff) encounter — chase sequence through Capitol

### Act 2: The Unraveling (2017-2018)
- Expand operations to London and Geneva
- Infiltrate **The Summit** (Davos) — learn full agenda
- Recruit first double agent
- Set bonus unlock: "The Whistleblower Network" (5-piece)

### Act 3: The Disruption (2019-2021)
- Pandemic arc — track **The Pill** (Big Pharma)
- **The Grid** (Big Tech) surveillance counter-ops
- Dark web marketplace missions
- Honeypot **Sleepy** (Biden) operation — prove cabal control
- Set bonus: "The Resistance" (7-piece vehicle set)

### Act 4: The Fracture (2022-2023)
- Cabal infighting: **Rock** vs **Gold Shield** proxy war
- Ukraine proxy conflict missions — **The Shield** (NATO) vs **The Czar** (Putin)
- Financial collapse missions — **The Vault** stability ops
- Infiltrate **The Lodge** (Bilderberg) — must have Legendary identity NFT
- End Act Boss: **Crooked** (Clinton) confrontation

### Act 5: The Fall (2024)
- All systems go — full cabal assault
- Final honey pot: **The Honey Queen** to capture **Fake** (Obama)
- **Pocahontas** (Warren) financial system breach
- **Sleepy** (Biden) removal operation
- **Fake Son** (Trudeau) digital ID network takedown
- Final boss: **The Lodge** council chamber — face all remaining cabal members
- Ending choices: **Reform**, **Destroy**, or **Join**

### Endgame (Post-2024)
- New content cycles: 2025, 2026 expansions
- Founder-driven narrative direction (Founder votes)
- Competitive seasons with exclusive Mythic NFTs
- Build completion race — first to Full Build gets permanent hall of fame

## The Mantle (Architect Access Level)
*The Mantle is an unlocked state earned through full collection completion — not a shortcut or cheat.*

**Prerequisites to unlock The Mantle:**
- Complete all 7 NFT collection categories (Full Build — ~156 pieces)
- Complete all 5 Acts of the campaign
- Capture at least one Mystic honey pot
- Be among the first 1,000 Founders OR own a Founders Token

**The Mantle unlocks:**
- **Architect View** — See the full cabal org chart in real-time (every NPC's allegiance, every mission outcome)
- **Narrative Forking** — Make campaign-altering decisions that permanently affect ALL players' game world
- **Mission Creation** — Design and deploy custom missions for other players (reviewed by dev team)
- **The Triangle Chamber** — Exclusive highest-level safehouse
- **The Black Book** — Complete player directory, offline backup of all game data
- **One-shot veto** — Once per season, override any game mechanic outcome

**Responsibility:** The Mantle is architect-level access, not infinite power. A Mantle holder who abuses the system loses the privilege permanently.

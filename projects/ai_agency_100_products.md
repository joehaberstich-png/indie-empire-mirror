## Vision
A single AI agency website selling 100 AI-powered products/services, built and managed by the 9-agent OpenClaw team. Products range from automation bots to AI tools — with the ClickBank affiliate empire and quantum bots as anchor products.

## Product Categories (High Level)
1. **AI Automation Bots** — e.g., Quantum Bots, trading bots, workflow bots
2. **AI Content Tools** — writing, video scripting, social media automation
3. **AI Agency Services** — done-for-you affiliate campaigns, bridge page builds
4. **AI SaaS Products** — tools sold via the agency website
5. **AI Consulting/Setup** — white-label agent deployment

## Architecture
- **Site:** Agency landing page → individual product pages → checkout/onboarding
- **Products rollout:** 10 per week → 100 in 10 weeks
- **Agent team maps:**
  - **Marketing Boss + Content Writer:** Website copy, product descriptions, blog
  - **Ad Analyst:** Traffic strategy, ad spend per product
  - **Operations Boss + Research Scout:** Market research, competition for each product
  - **Data Analyst:** Sales tracking, KPI dashboards
  - **Customer Success Boss + Support + Review Manager:** Client onboarding, tickets, reviews

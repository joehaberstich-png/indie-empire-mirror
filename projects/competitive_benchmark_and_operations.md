### Compliance Framework — Every Agent, Every Message

```
FTC ENDORSEMENT GUIDES (16 CFR Part 255)
├── Every affiliate link → "Some links may be affiliate links"
├── Clear disclosure — not buried in fine print
├── "Material connection" statement if paid promotion
├── No fake reviews or testimonials
├── No unsubstantiated claims ("make $10K/day")
└── Compliance check before every automated post

GDPR (EU — All Top 20 Countries)
├── Cookie consent banner
├── Data processing register
├── Right to erasure workflow
├── Data portability
├── Breach notification within 72 hours
└── DPO contact available

NFT/Crypto Legal (US + EU)
├── SEC registration review (Howey Test)
│   ├── Investment of money? N/A — subscription, not securities
│   ├── Common enterprise? N/A — NFTs are utility, not pooled
│   ├── Expectation of profits? Disclaimed — gameplay utility
│   └── Effort of others? Game content is player-driven
├── FinCEN registration (if applicable)
├── OFAC sanctions screening (player KYC)
├── Tax reporting (US 1099, EU DAC7)
└── Anti-money laundering policy

GAMING LICENSES
├── Age verification (18+ mandatory)
├── Loot box disclosure (if applicable)
├── Gambling compliance (₡CC cannot be cashed out)
└── Country-specific gaming laws (DE, UK, AU strictest)
```

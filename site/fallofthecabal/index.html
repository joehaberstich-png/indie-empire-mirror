◆ Narrative

The Truth is in the Details

Set between 2016 and 2024. Every character is a real-world figure with a codename. Every mission rooted in actual events. Every choice has consequences.

📜 The Campaign

You discover an encrypted hard drive containing documents that trace the largest hidden power network in human history. Your handler — known only as The Gardener — needs someone on the inside. Someone willing to follow the money, break the rules, and expose the truth.

What starts as a simple data recovery mission becomes an 8-year operation spanning 15 global cities, 5 major cabal factions, and a final confrontation that will determine the future of world order.

ACT I · 2016

🔍 The Awakening

Track cabal money flows through D.C. First honeypot. End Act Boss: Pencil Neck.

ACT II · 2017-18

🌍 The Unraveling

Expand to London and Geneva. Infiltrate The Summit (Davos). Recruit your first double agent.

ACT III · 2019-21

💊 The Disruption

Pandemic arc. Track Big Pharma. Counter Big Tech surveillance. Dark web operations.

ACT IV · 2022-23

⚔️ The Fracture

Cabal infighting. Ukraine proxy war. Bilderberg infiltration. End Act Boss: Crooked.

ACT V · 2024

💥 The Fall

The Honey Queen operation. Final boss: The Lodge council chamber. Three endings: Reform, Destroy, or Join.
